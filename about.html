ABOUT US

Welcome to our architectural firm, where creativity meets functionality, and dreams take shape in concrete, glass, and steel. Our commitment is to craft spaces that inspire, innovate, and endure. Here's what sets us apart.

“Architecture should speak of its time and place, but yearn for timelessness”

At ARCH we strive for quality, which is why we always use a construction team. This means that we work intensively on the plan and design before the start of construction. We bring together an expert team of professionals, including architects, engineers, project managers and craftsmen, to work together on your construction project. With our combined experience and knowledge we can make suggestions regarding costs, quality and efficiency in the construction plan.

At ARCH you will be completely unburdened during the entire construction process. From acquiring building land and arranging all permits, to the design of your home and its detailed furnishings. Thanks to our years of experience in building homes, we have good connections with various parties that are important in the construction of your home. This includes architects, developers and real estate agents. If desired, we advise and guide you in choosing the parties that suit you.
